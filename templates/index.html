Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

NCSC, Nova South

The National Cyber Security Centre (NCSC) has been kind enough to let us use their conference centre, boasting a capacity of 64 and a break-out space. It's located a stone's throw from Victoria train station (and a slightly more impressive throw from the coach station), London.

F.A.Q

  • Who should attend this event?

    This event is first and foremost for deliverers of digital products and services, as well as those who oversee their delivery. Nerds assemble!

  • Am I guaranteed a ticket if I apply?

    Unfortunately, the venue has a limited capacity, so we can't guarantee tickets. If we have a number of applications from the same team or area, we may have to ask that team to decide who comes along to represent them.

  • What do I do about paying for travel and subsistence?

    For future budgets, we hope to be able to provide some sort of T&S budget, but as this is the first one we're working on a zero-budget basis. To that end, you'll have to sort out your own T&S, unfortunately. If your local area will not pay for you to come, let us know and we'll see if anything can be done, but no promises!

  • What if I find out I can't make it on the day?

    Given the limited spaces, we're expecting to have a waiting list for this event. As such, please let us know as soon as possible if you can't make it, so that we can offer your ticket to someone else.

  • What on earth does "fac quod faciendum est" mean?

    Well, it was originally going to say "get shit done", but we figured people might not want that on public display. Instead, we've gone for this quotation from Seneca, which translates as "do what must be done".
